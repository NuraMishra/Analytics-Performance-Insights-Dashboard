# Analytical Dashboard

 Problem Statement
Marketing campaigns across multiple partners and regions are generating high spend, but performance efficiency (installs, conversions) is inconsistent. The goal is to identify inefficiencies and optimize budget allocation.

 Objective
Analyze marketing spend vs installs performance

Identify high and low performing partners

Evaluate regional performance differences

Optimize campaign efficiency (CPI, CPC)

 Tools Used
Power BI

Excel (Data Cleaning)

 Key Analysis
KPI Tracking: Total Spend, Installs, CPI, CPC, CTR

Temporal Analysis of Spend vs Installs

Partner-wise Performance Comparison

Region-wise Performance Analysis

 Key Insights
Partner 5 delivers the highest installs with significantly lower spend, making it the most cost-efficient partner.

Certain partners show high spend but relatively low installs, indicating inefficient budget utilization and higher CPI.

NY market generates the highest installs, suggesting strong user acquisition potential.

A noticeable dip in both spend and installs around Week 31 indicates possible campaign pause or reduced activity.

Increased spend does not always lead to proportional growth in installs, highlighting diminishing returns in some campaigns.

 Business Recommendations
Reallocate budget from underperforming partners to high-efficiency partners like Partner 5

Investigate high-spend, low-performance partners to reduce CPI

Scale campaigns in high-performing markets like NY

Maintain consistent campaign activity to avoid performance dips

Optimize spend strategy to avoid diminishing returns
